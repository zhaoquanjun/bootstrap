The Introduction
of SHIHUA company

“Suzhou Shihua New Material Technology Co., Ltd” was established in 2010 with the total investment of 160 million RMB. Shihua is a Hi-tech Enterprise, professional in the R&D and manufacturing of adhesive tape, protective films and other new materials. As a company in the chemical industry we are aware of our responsibility for people and the environment. The whole company regards Loyalty,pragmatic, integrity, happiness, gratitude, innovation as the core values, providing a self-fulfilling platform for employees.
